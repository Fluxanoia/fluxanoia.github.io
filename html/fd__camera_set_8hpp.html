Enumerator
FD_CAMERA_TRAN_INSTANT 

Instantly switches focus to the next FD_Camera.

FD_CAMERA_TRAN_SMOOTH 

Tweens the current FD_Camera onto the next one then switches focus to the next one. The original FD_Camera will have the dimensions and position of the new one.

FD_CAMERA_TRAN_SMOOTH_PRESERVED 

Tweens a temporary intermidiary FD_Camera onto the next one then switches focus to the next one. The original FD_Camera will not have its dimensions changed.
